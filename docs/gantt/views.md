The Gantt chart supports 6 timeline zoom levels.

## Available Views

| View    | Unit     | Best For             |
| ------- | -------- | -------------------- |
| Hour    | 1 hour   | Day-level scheduling |
| Day     | 1 day    | Short-term projects  |
| Week    | 1 week   | Medium-term planning |
| Month   | 1 month  | Long-term roadmaps   |
| Quarter | 3 months | Strategic overview   |
| Year    | 1 year   | Annual planning      |
